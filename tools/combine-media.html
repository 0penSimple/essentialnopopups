Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • The new audio is mixed with the video's existing sound. To replace the sound entirely, use the Remove audio tool first.
  • Start together — audio starts at the beginning of the video and stops when it ends or the video ends, whichever comes first.
  • End together — audio is delayed so it finishes exactly when the video does. Only shown when audio is shorter than the video.
  • The output keeps the video's original format and quality — only the audio track is changed.
  • You can also drop a static image (JPG, PNG, WebP) instead of a video. The image will be held for the full duration of the audio and exported as an MP4 — useful for turning a podcast or song into a shareable video.
