Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Trimming uses stream copy — no re-encoding means it's nearly instant and there's no quality loss.
  • Cuts are made at the nearest keyframe, so the actual cut point may be a fraction of a second off from what you set.
  • Drag the handles on the timeline, or type exact times in the start/end fields (format: 0:00).
  • FFmpeg (~20MB) loads once on first use and is cached for future visits.
