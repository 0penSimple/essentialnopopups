Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Unlike trimming, conversion re-encodes the video — this takes time. A 5-minute video may take several minutes to convert.
  • MP4 (H.264) is the most compatible format — it works on virtually every device and platform.
  • WebM produces smaller files and is ideal for web use, but may not play on older devices.
