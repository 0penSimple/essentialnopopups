Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your PDF is never uploaded anywhere.
  • Each page becomes one image file, all bundled into a ZIP for easy download.
  • JPG is smaller and better for photos. PNG is lossless and better for text or diagrams. WebP gives the best quality-to-size ratio.
  • Higher resolution means larger file sizes but sharper images — 150 DPI is good for screen use, 300 DPI for print.
