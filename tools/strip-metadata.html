Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your image is never uploaded anywhere.
  • GPS coordinates highlighted in yellow are the most sensitive — they can reveal exactly where a photo was taken.
  • This tool removes standard EXIF metadata. Some AI-generated images may contain invisible watermarks embedded in the pixels themselves, which cannot be removed this way.
  • The image quality and dimensions are fully preserved — only the hidden data layer is removed.
  • PNG files have less metadata than JPG by default, but can still contain author and software information.
