Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your images are never uploaded anywhere.
  • GPS location fields are highlighted in yellow — these are the most sensitive to remove before sharing.
  • Stripping metadata re-exports the image through a canvas — the visual quality is preserved but the file size may change slightly.
  • PNG files keep their transparency. JPG files are re-encoded at 95% quality.
