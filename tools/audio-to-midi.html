Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Piano recordings give the best results. Single-melody instruments (guitar, voice, violin) give decent results. Complex multi-instrument music may produce noisy output.
  • This tool supports clips up to 60 seconds. For longer recordings, split them into 60-second segments and convert each one — then merge the resulting MIDI files in your DAW.
  • Transcription takes roughly half the duration of the audio — a 60-second clip takes about 30 seconds to process. The page may feel unresponsive during this time, which is normal.
  • The AI model (~15MB) loads once on first use and is cached by your browser for future visits.
  • Video files are supported — the audio track is extracted automatically before transcription.
  • Output is a standard MIDI file you can open in any DAW (GarageBand, Ableton, Logic, FL Studio etc.).
