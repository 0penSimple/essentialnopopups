Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • MP3 is smaller and works everywhere. WAV is lossless and better for professional use.
  • If a video has no audio track the extraction will fail — this can happen with screen recordings or AI-generated videos.
  • FFmpeg (~20MB) loads once on first use and is cached by your browser for future visits.
  • Extraction time depends on file size — large video files may take a minute or more.
