Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your file is never uploaded anywhere.
  • Each page becomes its own PDF file, all bundled into a ZIP for easy download.
  • Need specific page ranges for free? Split first, then use our Merge PDFs tool to recombine exactly the pages you want.
  • Password-protected PDFs cannot be split — remove the password first.
