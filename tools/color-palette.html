Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your images are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Pixel picker: hover over any part of the image to see the color under your cursor. Click to save it to your picked colors list. A magnifier loupe helps with precise picking on detailed images.
  • Alt + click on any pixel to set it as the source color for replacement (Premium).
  • Colors are shown in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats. Click any color to copy its HEX code.
  • The dominant colors palette is extracted automatically using a clustering algorithm that finds the most representative colors in the image.
  • Color replacement (✦ Premium): pick a source color, a replacement color, and preview the result instantly. Use the tolerance slider to control how broadly similar pixels get replaced. The Match by setting controls how the matching works — Color matches by hue only and preserves original shading (best for most cases), Exact uses strict RGB matching for precise control, and Shade matches both hue and lightness together for selective replacements.
