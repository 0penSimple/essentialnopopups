Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your image is never uploaded anywhere.
  • 82% quality is the sweet spot for JPG — files are typically 40–60% smaller with no visible difference.
  • PNG compression is less effective than JPG or WebP — quality is fully preserved, but savings are typically more modest. Consider converting to WebP for the best results.
  • Use "by target size" if you need to fit an upload limit, like an email attachment or profile picture.
