Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • The PDF preserves the visual appearance of your document — formatting, images, tables, and layout all come through. The output is image-based, so text in the PDF will not be selectable or searchable.
  • Fonts that are not installed on your device may be substituted with a similar system font. The layout will still be correct but the exact typeface may differ slightly.
  • Complex layouts such as multiple columns, text boxes, and precise floating images generally convert well. Very large or complex documents may take a moment to process.
  • Password-protected Word documents cannot be converted. Remove the password first, then re-upload.
  • Files over 10MB may slow down your browser during conversion. For very large documents, consider splitting them first.
