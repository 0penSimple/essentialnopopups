Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your images are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Each image becomes one page in the PDF, in the order you set.
  • Fill page crops the image to cover the full page. Fit with margins keeps the full image with white space around it.
  • PNG transparency becomes white in the PDF — PDFs don't support transparent backgrounds.
  • Thumbnails are small and won't visually reflect quality or margin changes — the downloaded PDF will show the exact settings you chose.
