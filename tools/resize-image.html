Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your image is never uploaded anywhere.
  • Upscaling beyond the original size will look blurry — no browser tool can add real detail.
  • PNG keeps transparency. JPG and WebP do not — transparent areas become white.
  • For the smallest file size at good quality, choose WebP.
