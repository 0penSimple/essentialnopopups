Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Drag the files to set the order they play in the final audio.
  • Files with the same format and quality combine almost instantly with no quality loss.
  • Files with different formats are automatically re-encoded to match — this works, but takes a little longer.
  • This tool joins files back to back — there are no fade or crossfade transitions between them.
