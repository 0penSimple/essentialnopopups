Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • GIFs are limited to 256 colours per frame — videos with rich gradients or complex scenes may look washed out.
  • GIF files are much larger than video files for the same content. A 5-second clip can easily be 5–15MB.
  • Free tier supports clips up to 5 seconds. Premium up to 15.
  • Longer clips are trimmed from the start automatically.
