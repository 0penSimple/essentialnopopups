Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your images are never uploaded anywhere.
  • GIF supports a maximum of 256 colors per frame — photos may look slightly different than the originals.
  • Drag frames to reorder them before creating the GIF.
  • Larger images and more frames means longer processing time — be patient for big batches.
