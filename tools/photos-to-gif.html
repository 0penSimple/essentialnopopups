Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your images are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Drag the frames to set the order they appear in the GIF.
  • Larger images make larger GIF files — use the size setting to reduce file size.
  • GIFs are limited to 256 colours per frame, so photos with lots of colour variation may look slightly different.
