Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your files are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Drag the files to set the order they appear in the merged PDF.
  • Password-protected PDFs cannot be merged — remove the password first.
  • The merged file keeps all pages from every PDF in the order you set.
