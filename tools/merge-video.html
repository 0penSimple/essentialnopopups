Good to know

  • Everything happens in your browser — your videos are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Drag the clips to set the order they appear in the final video.
  • Clips with the same format, size and frame rate combine almost instantly with no quality loss.
  • Clips with different formats are automatically re-encoded to match — this works, but takes longer.
  • This tool does straight cuts only — there are no fade or crossfade transitions between clips.
