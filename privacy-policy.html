Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how EssentialBits (essentialbits.pro) handles information when you use our tools and services.

What we collect

We do not collect, store, or process any files you upload to our tools. All file processing happens entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers except where explicitly stated (such as the YouTube downloader, which uses a server to fetch video information).

If you sign up for our Premium early access list, we collect your email address via Formspree. This email is used solely to notify you when Premium launches and to send your discount code. We do not share your email with third parties.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display banner advertisements on our site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting aboutads.info.

Cookies

We do not set our own cookies. Google AdSense may set cookies on your device for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

Our site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — email signup processing. Privacy Policy
  • Railway — server hosting for the YouTube downloader. Privacy Policy
  • jsDelivr / CDN libraries — open source JavaScript libraries served via CDN.

Your files

All tools on this site process your files locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never shared. The only exception is the YouTube downloader, which sends a YouTube URL (not your files) to our server to retrieve video information and download the file on your behalf.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at essentialsnopopups@gmail.com.
